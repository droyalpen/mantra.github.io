Debt Recovery

Our firm has developed a strong and results-driven practice in the area of liquidated money demands and debt recovery, representing both individual and corporate creditors in pursuing unpaid debts through the legal process. We recognize that under Nigerian law, self-help is neither acceptable nor effective. As such, we are committed to upholding the rule of law by employing diligent, strategic, and lawful measures to recover debts on behalf of our clients.

With a deep understanding of debt enforcement procedures, our lawyers are well-versed in initiating and prosecuting debt recovery actions before the appropriate courts. We are skilled in seeking interim remedies, including applications for the attachment of the debtor’s moveable and immoveable assets, to secure our clients’ interests pending the final determination of the substantive claim.

In situations involving corporate debtors, we may initiate winding-up proceedings in accordance with the provisions of the Companies and Allied Matters Act (CAMA), either as a standalone remedy or alongside applications for summary judgment—particularly where the debt is undisputed or clear-cut.

Our approach combines legal precision with commercial awareness, ensuring that recovery efforts are both effective and compliant with all procedural and regulatory requirements. We work closely with our clients to assess the most efficient course of action and pursue remedies that safeguard their financial interests.
