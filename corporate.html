Corporate & Commercial Regulatory

Our team brings to bear a wealth of expertise and experience in corporate affairs and regulatory matters for companies with local and foreign participation. We are determined in offering highly professional and tailored- services in rendering legal advice and opinions for complex and non complex corporate matters involving incorporation of new businesses, and non-business organizations, obtaining of requisite licenses, business permits and expatriate quota, and other regulatory registrations as well as post-incorporation filings and corporate due-diligence whilst maintaining confidentiality.

The firm renders legal service on all aspects of operations in this field of law including but not limited to the following:

  • The choice, structure and registration of corporate entities.
  • Drafting of simple and complex agreements and legal documentations of all kinds.
  • Post Incorporation services/ Company restructuring and winding up.
  • Enterprise and business name registration and post registration services.
  • Corporate search.
